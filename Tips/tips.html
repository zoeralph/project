> Home Teaching 101 - Our Top Tips

  1. Maintain a relaxed, engaging & non-judgemental tone.
  2. Encourage active participation by active questioning to encourage discussion & keep children engaged.
  3. Ask children to explain concepts to you to consolidate their understanding.
  4. Gradually introduce information through smaller, ‘bite-sized’ sections.
  5. Incorporate the material into real-life scenarios or situations.
  6. Set clear goals, ground rules, & expectations at the beginning, so children know what they are working towards.
  7. Aim to keep the conversation focused on the course content so children don’t lose focus.
  8. Talk less & listen more.
  9. Act as a guide, rather than as an instructor.
  10. Adapt to your child’s learning preferences by asking for feedback.

> Tips For Ensuring Your Child's Wellbeing

  1. Play fun games & exercises.
    • Try playing sequencing games, spot the difference or memory games.
  2. Prepare a distraction-free environment.
    • Ambience – Soft instrumental music is ideal for concentration.
    • Gadgets – Remove all distractions & gadgets.
    • Reachable material – Keep everything that is required at hand.
  3. Maintain a healthy diet.
    • Sugary foods make a child sluggish while food rich in proteins aids concentration.
  4. Try to establish a solid routine.
    • This will allow your child to recognise when they need to study & can help with wellbeing.
  5. Ensure they have a steady sleep schedule keep your child healthy & focused.
    • Power naps can increase a child’s concentration & improve their wellbeing.
  6. Understand your child’s method of learning.
    • Visual – Try making flashcards, drawing or mind-maps.
    • Auditory – Try reading to your children or listening to music.
    • Kinaesthetic – Try practical demonstrations.
  7. Spend one-on-one time with your child outside of teaching time.
    • Because they are away from their friends, they may need extra support.
  8. Make time for physical exercise & fun activities.
    • Exercise will help improve your child’s mood & maintain their focus.
  9. Use energy effectively for better concentration & wellbeing.
    • Always start your child on tougher activities during their high energy time.
  10. Practice deep breathing & imagery to keep your child relaxed & calm.
    • Try simple relaxation techniques such as deep breathing or yoga for children.
